Water Definitions

Water Level:

This information can be assessed by the observer, gleaned from locals, or checked with the real time hydrometric information collected by Environment Canada at selected river stations in BC.


Outside of Normal Bank: The river or lake has gone over its bank and potentially into a floodplain.

Normal for the Season: Rivers and lakes have seasonal fluctuations in flow and level. Choose this option if the water body is within the normal range for the season in which you are working.

Low for the Season: The river or lake is lower than usual for the season.

High/Mid/Low Tide: On an estuary be aware of the tide cycle. Choose the closest tide to the beginning of your session.
